INDIAN VALLEYS

Journey to Indian Valleys

India, a land of enchantment and diversity, boasts a tapestry of landscapes that weave together to create a mesmerizing mosaic of natural beauty.

Nestled within this kaleidoscope of terrain are valleys that stand as epitomes of serene magnificence, each offering a unique blend of breathtaking vistas, rich cultural experiences, and unparalleled natural wonders.

KASOL

Known as 'Mini Israel' of India

Explore More

The charming village of Kasol is located in the captivating Parvati Valley near Kullu in Himachal Pradesh. Also known as 'Mini Israel' in India, you will notice many Israeli backpackers, restaurants serving Israeli food and signboards written in Hebrew language in every nook and corner of the village.

LEH-LADAKH

The Land of High Passes

Explore More

Leh-Ladakh forms a huge chunk of the cold desert in the Northern part of the Great Indian Himalayas. Rich natural beauty, stunning landscapes and beautiful Buddhist Monasteries, makes this place an ideal location to spend some days of your life connecting with nature.

TIRTHAN VALLEY

Gateway to Great Himalayan National Park

Explore More

A small village that seems to have directly come out from a fairy tale, Tirthan Valley is an ultimate delight and paradise for nature lovers and holiday seekers. The valley is located in the Kullu district of Himachal Pradesh and is surrounded with thick woods, lofty Himalayan peaks and striking apple orchards.
